rescaleAndRepulse

The rescaleAndRepulse function rescales the oscilloscope to take better advantage of the 8-bit ADC. It does not use the waveforms that are currently on the oscilloscope, but rather a set that is passed from the calling function. It assumes that the runDoublePulseTest has already been called.
